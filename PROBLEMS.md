# PROBLEMS

## Current Known Limitations

- Hosted/LAN operation is not the primary supported mode for the initial public seed. Run locally on `127.0.0.1` unless you are deliberately testing a controlled deployment.
- The public repository has limited checklist and capability examples due to the policy of omiting private/customer asset packs and early Whisper/VUI assets. Any examples added here must be generic and reviewed before commit.
- The compatibility/conformance story is intentionally early. Use the included tests as implementation regression tests, not as a complete third-party certification suite.
